# Code of conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Kelta welcoming and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, appearance, experience level or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific and constructive.
- Critique ideas and evidence, not people.
- State assumptions and disclose relevant conflicts of interest.
- Accept correction and help make technical discussions accessible.
- Prioritize the safety and well-being of the community.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation and publication of private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior violates these expectations. When the repository is published, confidential reports should be sent through the private maintainer contact listed in the repository security settings.
